In this course students will learn the procedures, protocols and best practices that a DevOps
engineer brings to a workplace environment to increase productivity. Analyze and evaluate
various developer operation implementations available and implement hands-on work using
industry standard toolkit. Create solutions to complex business problems by adhering to the core
responsibilities of DevOps


This course will equip students with the skills to effectively integrate DevOps practices into real-world environments, focusing on enhancing collaboration between development and operations teams. Through hands-on projects, students will gain experience with industry-standard tools for automation, continuous integration, and continuous delivery. By the end of the course, students will be able to design and implement DevOps solutions that address complex business challenges, optimize workflows, and drive productivity in modern software development settings.
