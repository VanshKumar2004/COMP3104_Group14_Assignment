Why choose George Brown College?

Located in one of Canada’s major economic centers, Toronto offers students a dynamic environment for learning. George Brown College has three campuses—St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront—situated in the heart of downtown, giving students hands-on experience in a vibrant, real-world setting.

We leverage our location to give students a competitive edge in the job market by partnering with leading companies and organizations. These partners contribute to our academic programs by advising on curriculum, offering field education opportunities, and providing employment pathways. By aligning education with industry needs, our students develop the sought-after skills needed to succeed in today’s workforce.
